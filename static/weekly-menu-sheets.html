      Planning Rules Used

      • Plate style: mostly non-starchy vegetables, a solid protein, and only a small high-fiber carb when it fits.
      • No naked carbs: fruit, beans, corn, rice, or bread should be paired with protein/fat/fiber.
      • Breakfasts are protein-forward so the day starts steady.
      • First lunch is fresh, not leftovers; leftovers are used later only when they still feel intentional.
      • Keep dinner family-friendly, but make the default base salad, greens, cauliflower rice, or extra vegetables.
